# global-sales-data-analysis

**Description:**
Analysis of a global retail company's sales data from both online and offline stores. The dataset includes three tables:

- events.csv — sales over several years
- products.csv — product categories and codes
- countries.csv — countries, regions, and codes

**Objective:**
Clean, analyze, and visualize the data to uncover actionable business insights.

**Project Steps:**

- Data Overview: Understand dataset structure, key columns, and relationships between tables.
- Data Cleaning: Handle missing values, fix data types, remove duplicates, and detect anomalies.
- Data Analysis & Visualization: merge tables and create key metrics, analyze sales by product category, country/region, and sales channel (online/offline), examine order-to-shipment time and its effect on profit, explore sales trends over time and by day of the week to identify seasonal patterns.
- Reporting: Compile a detailed Google Colab report with code, visualizations, and business-oriented conclusions.
- Outcome: A complete data analytics case study demonstrating data cleaning, analysis, visualization, and business insight generation.
